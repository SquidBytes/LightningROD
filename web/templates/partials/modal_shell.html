{# Modal Shell Pattern ================== The modal shell div IS the fixed overlay (backdrop + centering). Usage: Copy this pattern into the page. HTMX loads the modal partial into the inner content div. The partial starts directly with the card div (no fixed inset-0 wrapper of its own). Shell classes: hidden fixed inset-0 z-[60] flex items-start justify-center pt-8 pb-8 overflow-y-auto bg-black/60 Click-outside-to-close: onclick="if(event.target === this) closeXxxModal()" The card partial must have onclick="event.stopPropagation()" to prevent the backdrop click handler from firing when clicking inside the modal. #}
